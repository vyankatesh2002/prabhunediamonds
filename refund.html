Return & Refund Policy

We're committed to ensuring you have a hassle-free shopping experience. If you're not completely satisfied, here's how we can help.

Key Policy Points

  • Return Window: 7–14 days from delivery dateIMPORTANT
  • Condition: Items must be unused, in original packaging with all tags attached
  • Non-Returnable: Personalized items, perishable goods, and intimate apparel
  • Refund Processing: 5–7 business days after we receive and inspect your return
  • Shipping Costs: Original shipping is non-refundable unless the return is due to our error
  • Exchanges: Defective/damaged items will be replaced at no additional cost

How to Initiate a Return

  • Contact our support team via email or phone within the return window
  • Pack the item securely in its original packaging with all accessories
  • Ship to our return address (provided by our team) using a trackable service
  • Keep your tracking number until you receive confirmation of receipt
